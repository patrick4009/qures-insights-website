[ FAQ ]
How fast do I get results?+

Most questions reach a usable, benchmarked result in around 48 hours. Larger or tightly-targeted audiences can take a little longer; you watch responses stream in live.

Who actually answers?+

Real, opted-in members of the QuRes app who earn points for answering. They're engaged everyday people, matched to your targeting, not professional survey-takers.

Is it anonymous and GDPR-compliant?+

Yes, by design. You only ever see grouped trends and percentages. A response is never tied to a name, and members can export or delete their data at any time.

How precisely can I target?+

Age group, topics and interests, and location down to a country, its cities, or a radius around a postcode. Choose one country and you can target a mileage radius.

What answer formats can I use?+

All ten: multiple choice, yes/no, 0–10 scale, agree-to-disagree, dropdown, ranking, brand and product comparison, short text and open feedback.

What do I get at the end?+

A branded PDF report with distributions, segment breakdowns by age and region, a per-location response heatmap, a benchmarked QuRes Insight Score and editable recommendations.

How much does it cost?+

It depends on your audience, sample size and cadence. There's no public price list, every brief is different. See pricing or book a demo.
