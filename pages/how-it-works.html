The whole loop, four steps.

[ The loop ]
01
Target
Pick who answers, age group, location down to the city (or a radius around a postcode), plus topics and interests. We match an anonymous, opted-in audience that fits.
02
Ask
Write one clear question and choose from ten answer formats, multiple choice, yes/no, 0–10 scale, agree-disagree, dropdown, ranking, brand and product comparison, short and open text. Set the reward.
03
Collect
Members answer in seconds inside the QuRes app and earn points. Responses stream back live, with answer-time and drop-off tracked per question, and low-quality answers screened out.
04
Report
See distributions, segment breakdowns by age and region, a per-location response heatmap, and a benchmarked QuRes Insight Score, exported as a branded PDF, ready to share.
